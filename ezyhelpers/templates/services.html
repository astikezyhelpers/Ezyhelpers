FAQS

Common Questions

Everything you need to know about our services. Can't find an answer? Contact us anytime.

All our helpers undergo a rigorous 5-step vetting process:

  • Comprehensive background checks
  • Reference verification (minimum 2 professional references)
  • Skills assessment and practical testing
  • In-depth in-person interviews
  • Specialized training program completion

We only work with the top 20% of applicants to ensure quality service for your family.

Your satisfaction is our top priority. Our guarantee includes:

  • First 2 weeks: Free replacement if you're not completely satisfied
  • Ongoing support: We'll work with you to resolve any issues
  • Mediation services: If any concerns arise, we facilitate communication
  • Additional training: We can provide extra training for your helper at no cost

Our pricing is transparent and varies based on:

  • Service type: Live-in, part-time, or specialized care
  • Experience level: Entry-level to premium professional helpers
  • Responsibilities: Standard duties vs. additional requirements
  • Location: Metro vs. regional areas

Typical costs:
Placement fee: ₹500-₹1500 (one-time)
Helper salary: ₹20-₹35 per hour depending on experience
Contact us for a personalized quote based on your needs.

Our standard process timeline:

  • Initial consultation: 1-2 days
  • Candidate matching: 3-5 business days
  • Interviews: Scheduled at your convenience
  • Onboarding: 2-3 days after selection

For standard requests, most families have their helper start within 1-2 weeks of initial contact. We also offer:

  • Express service: Helper within 72 hours (+20% fee)
  • Temporary placements: Available immediately for short-term needs

Our support continues throughout your helper's employment:

  • Regular check-ins: Scheduled follow-ups to ensure satisfaction
  • 24/7 support line: Urgent matters handled anytime
  • Performance reviews: Optional formal evaluations
  • Training updates: Access to continuing education for your helper
  • Replacement guarantee: If your helper leaves, we find a replacement quickly
  • Mediation services: Help resolving any issues that arise
