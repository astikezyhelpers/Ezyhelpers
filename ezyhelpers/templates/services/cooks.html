Cuisines Our Cooks Specialize In

Our experienced home cooks in Bangalore can prepare a wide variety of cuisines, including:

North Indian Cooks

They are skilled in preparing dishes characterised by rich gravies, dairy products, and a variety of bread. Their cuisine includes popular items like butter chicken, paneer dishes, and various kinds of naan and rotis.

South Indian Cooks

These cooks excel in dishes that are typically rice-based, such as dosas, idlis, and sambhar. They use a lot of coconuts, curry leaves, and mustard seeds, offering a blend of flavours that are both spicy and tangy.

Chinese Cooks

Specialising in Chinese cuisine, these cooks are adept in stir-frying, steaming, and deep-frying techniques. They prepare dishes like noodles, fried rice, dim sum, and various meat and vegetable preparations, often flavoured with soy sauce, garlic, and ginger.

Continental Cooks

Focusing on European cuisine, Continental cooks are known for their expertise in dishes that are less spicy yet rich in flavours. They prepare a variety of pasta, steaks, salads, and baked goods, using ingredients like olive oil, wine, herbs, and cheese.
