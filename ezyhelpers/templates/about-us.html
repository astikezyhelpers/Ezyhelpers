About EzyHelpers

Transforming homes and lives through trusted domestic help services since 2010

OUR STORY

From Humble Beginnings to Industry Leaders

How a simple idea grew into India's most trusted domestic help provider

2010

Founded in a Small Apartment

After struggling to find reliable help for his aging parents, founder Rajesh Kumar started EzyHelpers with just 3 helpers and a vision to create a better way to connect families with domestic professionals.

2014

First Office & Training Center

With growing demand, we opened our first dedicated office and training facility, allowing us to implement our comprehensive 5-step vetting and training program that became our hallmark.

2018

National Expansion

After perfecting our model in Delhi, we expanded to 5 major cities across India, bringing our premium domestic help services to thousands more families.

Today

10,000+ Happy Families

Today, EzyHelpers is India's most trusted domestic help provider, serving over 10,000 families with a team of 500+ carefully vetted professionals across 12 cities.
